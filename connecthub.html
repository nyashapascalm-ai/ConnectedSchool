How it works

A connection that funds itself.

The whole setup is financed as an asset — so the school pays nothing upfront, and the community covers the cost.

1

Install

Solar panels, a Starlink dish and computers are set up at the school and financed by a loan.

2

Open the café

The community buys WiFi vouchers to get online — a real income stream from day one.

3

Collect the levy

A small ICT levy per pupil is collected through mobile money or cash at the counter.

4

Repay the loan

That income services the loan automatically. Once it's cleared, the income is the school's.
