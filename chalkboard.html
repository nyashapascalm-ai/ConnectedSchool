How it works

Set it up once, run the school all year.

Chalkboard sits with admin — not on every computer — and gives the head and bursar one view of the whole school.

1

Set up fees

Define fees and levies per class or per pupil, once, at the start of term.

2

Collect & record

Take fees by mobile money or cash — every payment lands against the right pupil.

3

Run the day

Mark attendance, update records, track assets and inventory as you go.

4

Report & account

Generate report cards and see the money in and out — no spreadsheets.
