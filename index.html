How it fits together

Lay the foundation, then build the house.

You can't run software at a school with no power and no internet. So ConnectHub goes in first — and pays for itself. On that foundation, Chalkboard runs the school and Dari teaches. One deployment, one loan, one connected school.

  • ConnectHub is the foundation — power, internet, and the income that repays the loan.
  • Chalkboard is the recurring platform that runs the school's money and admin.
  • Dari turns the connected lab into a place pupils actually learn.
